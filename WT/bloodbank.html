Before You Donate:
  • Stay hydrated
  • Eat healthy

#Instruction
1Drink plenty of water before donating blood.
2Eat a healthy meal before your visit.
3Minimum age for donation: 18 years.
4Minimum weight required: 50 kg.
5Gap between donations: 3 months for men, 4 months for women.
6Avoid alcohol and fatty foods 24 hours before donation.
7Inform the staff about any medication or recent illness.
8Carry a valid ID proof.
